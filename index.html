Brake Service 101 — simple guide

Brake Pads

  • Ceramic
  • Semi-Metallic
  • Organic

Ceramic: quiet, low dust. Semi-metallic: stronger bite; may be noisier. Organic: softer, budget option.

Example pads with shim kit & spring clips — hardware helps prevent noise and keeps pads moving freely.

Brake Rotors

  • Solid / Vented
  • Drilled
  • Slotted

Vented: most common; helps cooling. Drilled/Slotted: usually not needed for daily cars — use what your vehicle is designed for.

Example rotors — choose the type your vehicle is designed for.

Hardware (shims & clips)

New hardware helps pads slide smoothly and reduce noise. Replace worn hardware when installing new pads.
