About Me

My name is Juan Fernandez, and I’m the founder of HONESTECH Mobile Mechanic Service. I’ve been in the automotive repair industry for over 27 years — it’s not just my profession, it’s my passion.

After decades working in dealership environments, I wanted to bring that same level of quality and professionalism directly to people who need help the most. Not everyone has the ability to bring their car into the shop — maybe you’re home with kids, dealing with a disability, or just unable to get away from work.

That’s where HONESTECH was born — a mobile mechanic service that comes to you. My goal is to make auto repair convenient, transparent, and fair — with no hidden charges or inflated part costs. You provide the parts, and I’ll handle the rest — from replacement to repair.

Every job I take on is personal. I treat every car like my own and every customer like family. It’s not just about fixing cars — it’s about helping people stay safe and keep moving without the stress or hassle of a dealership visit.

“Honesty and quality shouldn’t cost extra — that’s what HONESTECH stands for.”

If you ever need help, advice, or a trusted mechanic who genuinely cares — I’m just a call or text away.
