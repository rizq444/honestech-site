About Me

My name is Juan Fernandez, and I’m the founder of HONESTECH Mobile Mechanic Service. I’ve been in the automotive repair industry for over 27 years it’s not just my profession, it’s my passion.

After decades in dealership environments, I wanted to bring that quality directly to people who can’t make it to a shop maybe you’re home with kids, dealing with a disability, or stuck at work. I come to you, on your schedule.

No hidden charges, no inflated parts costs. You provide the parts, and I’ll replace or repair them the right way.
