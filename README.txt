HOW TO APPLY THIS PACK

1) Open your site's styles.css and paste the contents of styles-additions.css at the very bottom.
2) In index.html:
   - Replace the existing hero card content with soft-card.html
   - Paste guides.html below your Services section
3) Upload the /img/ folder. These are SVG placeholders you can keep or replace.
