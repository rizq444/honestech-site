Why You Should Replace Your Engine Air Filter

The engine air filter is one of the simplest and most important parts of your car’s maintenance routine. It protects your engine by trapping dirt, dust, and debris before they can enter the combustion chamber.

Signs You Need a New Filter

  • Reduced fuel efficiency
  • Sluggish acceleration
  • Unusual engine sounds or hesitation
  • Dirty or dark filter surface

How Often Should You Replace It?

Most vehicles require replacement every 12,000–15,000 miles or once a year. However, if you drive in dusty or polluted environments, you may need to change it more often.

Benefits of Replacing It

  • Improved fuel economy
  • Better throttle response
  • Longer engine life
