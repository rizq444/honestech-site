Why Cabin Air Filter Replacement Matters

Breathe cleaner air and keep your HVAC working efficiently.

What the Cabin Filter Does

It cleans the air that comes through your HVAC system — trapping pollen, dust, and odors.

When to Replace

  • Every 12,000–15,000 miles or once a year
  • Musty smells or weak airflow
  • Visible dirt/debris on the filter

Why It Matters

  • Cleaner air for you and your family
  • Helps defog windows faster
  • Protects blower motor from dust buildup
