Vehicle Service Intervals — How Often Should You Maintain Your Car?

Your quick-reference maintenance schedule, adjusted for real-world driving.

General Maintenance Guide

  • Every 5k–7.5k miles: Engine oil & rotation (follow your manual + oil type)
  • 15k miles: Engine & cabin air filters check/replace
  • 30k miles: Brake fluid inspection, transmission fluid (if applicable), coolant check
  • 60k miles: Spark plugs (platinum/iridium), coolant service per manual
  • 100k+ miles: Timing components (if belt), accessory belt, water pump as needed

Driving conditions matter: short trips, extreme temps, towing, and dusty roads shorten intervals.
