Should You Add a Fuel Additive?

What they can (and can’t) fix — and how to use them wisely.

What Fuel Additives Do

Most are detergents or cleaners that help remove deposits in injectors and on intake valves.

When They Help

  • Rough idle or hesitation from light deposits
  • As preventative maintenance every 5,000–10,000 miles

Best Practices

  • Choose reputable brands
  • Follow the bottle’s dosage
  • They aren’t a fix for failing hardware
