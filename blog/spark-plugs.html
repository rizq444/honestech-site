When and Why to Replace Spark Plugs

Fresh plugs restore efficiency, power, and smooth starts.

Typical Lifespan

  • Copper: ~30k miles
  • Platinum: ~60k miles
  • Iridium: ~90k–120k miles

Symptoms of Worn Plugs

  • Rough idle or misfire
  • Hard starts
  • Poor MPG
  • Lack of power

Why Replace on Time

  • Protects ignition coils and catalytic converter
  • Restores efficiency and performance
