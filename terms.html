Terms & Conditions

Please read these terms before booking service with Honestech Mobile Mechanic Service.

1. Purpose

Honestech Mobile Mechanic Service (“Honestech”) provides convenient mobile vehicle maintenance and repair, and connects customers with trusted independent technicians as needed.

2. Independent Technicians

Technicians are independent contractors, not employees of Honestech. Each technician is responsible for the accuracy, quality, and completion of their work and assumes all liability related to services performed.

3. Customer Responsibilities

Please ensure the parts you purchase are correct for your vehicle (by VIN or verified year/make/model/engine). Provide a safe, accessible workspace suitable for mobile service.

4. Service Booking

Customers select a preferred date/time via the website. After review, Honestech will confirm the request and dispatch the job to an available technician if applicable.

5. Service Area & Technician Assignment

Middlesex County, NJ: When available, the owner of Honestech will personally perform the service. If unavailable, the job may be referred to a trusted independent technician.

Outside Middlesex County: Honestech will recommend and refer a trusted, approved independent technician who has agreed to Honestech’s quality and professionalism standards.

6. Pricing & Payment

  • Typical labor: $120 for the first hour, then $100 per hour thereafter (unless otherwise quoted).
  • Payment is due upon completion and is made directly to the technician unless otherwise arranged.
  • Stripe payments include a 3% processing fee.
  • Roadside services outside Middlesex County: $1 per mile from the county line (when applicable).

7. Additional Work / Upsells

If additional needs are identified during service, the technician will communicate before proceeding. Upsells may be handled privately between the technician and customer, or managed through Honestech for a 10% coordination fee.

8. Work Documentation

Technicians agree to take before-and-after photos (and short video if helpful) and provide copies to both the customer and Honestech within 24 hours of completion for quality and record-keeping.

9. Liability

All liability for workmanship and service rests with the technician performing the work. Honestech is a referral and scheduling service and is not liable for damages, injury, or loss arising from services performed.

10. Acknowledgment

By booking service, you acknowledge and agree to these terms, including the independent nature of technicians, your responsibility to provide correct parts, and the payment terms above.
