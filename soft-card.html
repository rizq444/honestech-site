Please double-check your parts

When purchasing parts, please make sure they match your vehicle by VIN or your exact year / make / model / engine.

Helpful tips

  • Brakes: pads + rotors (front or rear) and a hardware/shim kit
  • Oil change: correct oil grade & capacity + correct oil filter
  • Tune-up: spark plugs (right type & gap), engine air filter, cabin filter, wiper blades
